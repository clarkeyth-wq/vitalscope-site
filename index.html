FAQ
Quick answers
Does Vitalscope replace medical advice?
No. Vitalscope is for awareness and reflection. It can help you notice patterns, but it isn’t a medical device and doesn’t diagnose conditions. If something feels concerning, it’s always okay to speak with a clinician.
Do I need a wearable?
Not necessarily. If your phone or connected apps write data to Apple Health or Health Connect, Vitalscope can use it. Wearables can improve detail, but they’re optional.
How long does it take to learn my baseline?
Often you’ll see useful patterns within 1–2 weeks. Some insights become clearer with a bit more time, especially if your data is irregular.
Will it work with Samsung Health, Fitbit, Garmin?
If your provider syncs into Health Connect (Android) or Apple Health (iOS), then yes. Vitalscope reads what’s available in those hubs.
Is my data private?
Vitalscope is designed to minimize collection and keep control with you. We do not sell personal health information. (We can add a full Privacy Policy page later.)
