FAQ

A few quick answers.

Does Vitalscope give medical advice?

No. It highlights trends and gentle context from your own data. Any “pattern noticed” copy is descriptive — not diagnostic.

Will it shame me for a bad day?

No. Language stays neutral and supportive. The app focuses on steadiness, variation, and recovery pacing — without “fail” states.
