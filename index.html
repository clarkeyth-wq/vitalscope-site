How it works

1) Connect your data

Vitalscope reads signals from Apple Health or Health Connect—so it can use the data your devices already collect.

2) Learn your baseline

Over time, Vitalscope learns what “steady” looks like for you. The more consistent your data, the clearer the picture becomes.

3) Notice gentle shifts

When patterns drift, Vitalscope highlights it with context—so you can take a calmer, more informed look at what might be happening.

FAQ
Does Vitalscope replace medical advice?
No. Vitalscope is for awareness and reflection. It can help you notice patterns, but it isn’t a medical device and doesn’t diagnose conditions. If something feels concerning, it’s always okay to speak with a clinician.
Do I need a wearable?
Not necessarily. If your phone or connected apps write data to Apple Health or Health Connect, Vitalscope can use it. Wearables can improve detail, but they’re optional.
How long does it take to learn my baseline?
Often you’ll see useful patterns within 1–2 weeks. Some insights become clearer with a bit more time, especially if your data is irregular.
Will it work with Samsung Health, Fitbit, Garmin?
If your provider syncs into Health Connect (Android) or Apple Health (iOS), then yes. Vitalscope reads what’s available in those hubs.
Why is the tone so “calm” compared to other apps?
Many health tools unintentionally add pressure. Vitalscope is built to be steady and non-anxiety-inducing—more like a quiet mirror than a coach.
Is my data private?
Yes—Vitalscope is designed to minimize collection and keep control with you. We do not sell personal health information. (If you want, we can add a full Privacy Policy page later, but this one-page site keeps it simple for now.)
